About Me

I’m a Graphic Design student at Otis College of Art & Design. Los Angeles transplant from the Bay Area. Image and Printmaking are things that I really enjoy working on. Most of my work is very tongue and cheek and heavily references niche culture. I want to make work that makes people stop and question the intentions of the images and for me to help them explore a new way of perceiving content. Outside of design, I’m really passionate and inspired by old funk iconography and it ends up presenting itself in my work. Any more question, feel free to ask. \m/
